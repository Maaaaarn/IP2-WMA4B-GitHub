About our Restaurant

Our History

Our first location was at Dau, Mabalacat Pampanga, which is in front of S&R Dau. We are very happy to see our customers getting excited whenever they order and being curious about our BBC varieties (which we think that's the reason why they are still coming back to us even if we relocated!) We see their eyes brighten up as we serve them our specialties, and it really really brightens up our day, seeing our customer satisfied upon leaving. Until then, we keep on striving for the better, not just for our sake, but for our consistent returnees who have faith on our capabilities.

We took a big step by taking the opportunity to relocate to a place where people are active, loves to eat, and whom might be missing the moments of their lives to eat the most delicious steaks and burgers in the town of Angeles City!
